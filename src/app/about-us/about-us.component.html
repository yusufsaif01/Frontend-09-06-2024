Unleashing potential with cutting edge sports technology



YFTChain strives to establish an ecosystem that facilitates the interaction between football clubs, academies, and coaches for the purpose of player development, leveraging data and video analysis.



With a staggering 25 million children engaged in football, alongside a network of 20,000 coaches and over 10,000 football academies, India stands as a vibrant hub for the sport.
