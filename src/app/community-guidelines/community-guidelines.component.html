COMMUNITY GUIDELINES



YFTChain (Youth Football Technology Chain), a product of Decoding Youth Talent Private Limited, helps its users (football players, coaches, clubs and academies) to connect with one another, professionally and socially, to enable a robust footballing community, talent identification, scouting, talent showcasing through videos, photos, posts and using data for match and training analysis. To achieve the above-desired purpose, we have carefully drafted our community guidelines mentioned below and mandate all registered users to follow the community guidelines as well as the Terms of Use.

At YFTChain, we value and give utmost importance to freedom of speech and expression, the right to privacy of each user registered on our platform. Hence we expect and mandate all of our users to interact with other users on the platform in a way they would interact in a real-world scenario. All YFTChain users, before posting or publishing any content (text, photo, video) onto the platform, must think about how other people would react or feel if you conveyed to them the type of content you are about to post. Never publish any content related to another user or non-user you would have a problem with or not like if posted about you.

The community guidelines, as stated below, set out the standard for actions and behaviour that are restricted, prohibited, and strictly not allowed on the YFTChain platform. In the event where any user(s) is found to be guilty of performing any of the actions and behaviour stated below, appropriate action and redressal might be taken, including, but not limited to, removing the violative or indiscriminative or inappropriate content, informing the authorities and termination of your account as a registered user on the platform.

1. Be Professional

YFTChain requires its users to be professionally relevant and on the topic, which is Football. Users on the YFTChain platform should only share, upload information, videos, and any other data which helps them to promote themselves in the world of Football, an opportunity for gaining expertise learning, coaching, getting scouted, acquiring new footballing skills; and engaging in actions or behaviours that facilitate you and others to be more productive and successful.

2. Hate Speech and Discrimination

Do not post hateful or discriminatory content on YFTChain.

YFTChain requires its registered users to treat other users with the utmost respect, dignity, integrity, professionalism, and a feeling of empathy. YFTChain encourages users to participate in healthy discussions, dialogues, and expressions that do not intentionally harm any other user’s or person’s feelings or opinion or point of view on the platform. We have a strict policy and do not allow users to use any expressions and content can be perceived to be hateful, discriminatory, prejudiced, incite violence or hatred, create a divide amonggroups of individuals based on their religion, ethnicity, nationality, place of origin, language, caste, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, diseases.

YFTChain users must refrain from using any hateful, indiscriminate, abusive images, videos, or any other type of offensive content in their display name, profile bio, profile information, picture, header, posts, comments, achievements, messages, chats. Any content posted or created by the user found to be constructed and published to cause harassment to another user or person or group of individuals will be dealt with appropriate action and, if need be, will be reported to the respective authorities to be dealt with. YFTChain reserves the right to initiate appropriate legal action as permissible under the applicable law against users that post hateful or discriminatory content on YFTChain’s Platform.

3. Religiously Offensive Content

Be respectful of the religious beliefs of others.

All YFTChain users must be tolerant, respectful of any and every religious faith, notion, beliefs of other users, persons, individuals, or groups of individuals regardless of their views, opinions, beliefs, and whether they believe or agree or disagree with them or not. No content should be posted or published on the YFTChain platform that can potentially cause damage to the sentiment of other or ridicules or disrespects their religion or customs, or causes communal division.

4. Terrorism and Extremism

Do not post content that threatens, encourages, or glorifies terrorism.

YFTChain strictly prohibits and refrains its users from using any kind of information, content, videos, articles, posts, messages, comments, data, whether drafted, recorded, or published that implicitly or explicitly promotes, incites terrorism, secession, acts of violence against any user, individual, group of individuals, property, a particular community, religion, caste that threats the unity, integrity, defence, security or sovereignty of India, relations with foreign countries or insults another nation. YFTChain users shall not publish any content that incites or promotes or encourages other users to participate or take actions such as recruit, spread, and disseminate information on behalf of a terrorist or any individual, entity, or organisation deemed to be a threat to the defence, security or sovereignty of India, as notified by the Government of India. YFTChain does not permit any illegal, unlawful, seditious or terrorist organisation to join our platform, nor do we allow or will accept, or tolerate any individual affiliated with any such organisation in any manner.

5. Violent Content

Do not post content that threatens, depicts, or glorifies violence or encourages self-harm.

YFTChain does not allow its user to post any violent content that may or may not potentially cause theft, wrongful confinement, physical harm, mental harm, bodily harm, financial distress, or vandalism to any other user, individual, group of individuals indirectly or directly.

YFTChain Users must not and are forbidden to publish any content which relates to or depicts mass murder, severe physical violence, mental suffering, abduction, kidnapping, drug abuse, non-consensual sexually explicit content (e.g., pornographic or pedophilic content, content harming children, or revenge pornography, etc.), exploitation of children, adults, human trafficking, criminal activity, instructional weapon-making, suicide, any type of self-harming, prostitution, and torture. YFTChain has a zero-tolerance policy against any user who tries to glorify or celebrate any previously committed crimes that have caused physical, sentimental, and or mental distress to any user, individual, group of individuals, community, organisation.

6. Graphic, Obscene and Sexual Content

Do not post graphic, obscene, and/or sexual content on YFTChain.

YFTChain does not tolerate content that is obscene or sexually graphic or which could be seen to be inappropriate for some users. YFTChain does not allow pornographic or pedophilic content, explicit depictions of deceased persons, violent sexual acts including content depicting rape, and excessively gory imagery. YFTChain also has a zero-tolerance policy for any content which shows sexual content involving children or which may be otherwise harmful to children or revenge pornography. Any users found posting content of this nature will be immediately reported to the concerned authorities under the applicable law.

7. Cyberbullying

Be kind to others. Don’t be a bully.

YFTChain abstains its users from publishing any negative, harmful, false, or mean content in the form of posts, photos, videos, comments, text, audio, messages about someone else that can affect their physical, mental, sentimental, emotional well-being. Users must also not share personal or private information about other user(s), groups, communities, organisations that are embarrassing and/or humiliating and/or can damage someone’s reputation and/or goodwill. YFTChain considers cyberbullying to be unlawful and representing criminal behaviour. All users must not use any kind of abusive language on the platform and in particular towards any other user or organisation.

8. Invasion of Privacy

Be respectful of the privacy of others.

All registered YFTChain users are expected to refrain from posting or sharing any information or inciting the share or publication of personal and professional information of another person without prior written consent as it is considered to be invasive of their privacy.

9. Illegal Activities

Much like in the real world, don’t break the law when you use YFTChain.

YFTChain users are not allowed to publish any illegal content or promote illegal activity or entice other users to participate in unlawful activity. The activities include but are not limited to the purchase and sale of harmful substances, narcotics, prescription drugs, alcohol, tobacco, or any other commodity or item that is restricted to be traded between private individuals. . YFTChain is not a platform where any user can promote or provide access to gambling tournaments, lotteries, real money competitions, human or child trafficking, unorganised or organised violence, or other criminal activity.

YFTChain has a zero-tolerance policy for any content intended to explicitly or implicitly disrupt public law and order, prevents or misleads criminal investigation, or encourages other users to commit an offence.

YFTChain has a zero tolerance policy for any activities including, but not limited to “doping in sport” as defined under the National Anti-Doping Act, 2022 or any other activities punishable under applicable law.

10. Identity Theft and Impersonation

Identity theft is serious. Don’t impersonate any other person.

YFTChain users are not allowed to commit fraud or attempt to use the identifying personal or private information of another person, group, organisation, or private entity to deceive or mislead other users. Users are also not allowed to publish content that is meant to mislead about the origin of content. All users must

ensure that the information provided by them in their profile is accurate and is not falsified or misrepresented. YFTChain reserves the right to take down/delete/block the profiles or accounts of such users who commit impersonate other individuals, or attempt to use the identifying personal or private information of another person, group, organisation, or private entity to deceive or mislead other users. YFTChain reserves the right to report all such users to the concerned authorities as under the applicable law.

11. Misinformation, Fake News, and Fake profiles

Provide verifiable, credible, and real information.

YFTChain users must not post, publish, or share any unverified, misleading or false content. . YFTChain does not tolerate media that includes photoshopped pictures of doctored videos. We will not allow any content published, posted, or shared that could potentially affect people-centric processes. YFTChain does not allow any content or material that may influence or interfere with political elections in India conducted as per applicable Election Laws. YFTChain’s platform is meant for professional networking and development of the football industry, and hence we will not permit any information to be posted which interferes with the results of political elections..

12. Spamming, Scamming, and Phishing

Don’t use YFTChain to spam or scam others.

YFTChain does not allow its users to manipulate others or negatively affect their experience on the YFTChain through gratuitously repetitive messages, inappropriate, unwanted, amplified, unwanted, or other similar content. YFTChain users must not use the platform for sensationalising or capitalising unfortunate events for their own financial or commercial benefits or with an intent to harm another user, individual, or organisation. We encourage users to create their original, professional and exciting content to maximise their outreach and popularity to all stakeholders registered on the platform rather than using artificial ways to increase their popularity.

13. Intellectual Property Infringement

Respect the intellectual property of others.

YFTChain users must not use, publish or post any text, images, music, or videos or any other artistic works that others have copyright on. Users should not use trademarked brands or logos of organisations that have not authorised them to use or any other such proprietary rights that could result in infringement of their intellectual property rights. YFTChain users must post content on the platform that is not owned or can exclusively be used by another person unless the respective person or user has authorised and licensed such intellectual property to you .

14. Malicious Programmes

Don’t share malicious programs.

YFTChain users are not allowed to post, publish or share any content that contains malicious programs or tools which are specifically designed to delete, block, modify, copy data or to disrupt the experience of other users’ computers or performance of computers and/or computer networks affecting the functionality of the platform adversely.

15. Unwanted advances

Do not engage in unwanted advances.

YFTChain does not allow unwanted expressions of attraction, desire, requests for a romantic relationship, marriage proposals, sexual advances, or innuendo or lewd remarks. Do not use YFTChain to pursue romantic connections, ask for romantic dates, or provide sexual commentar
