o In order to the send a FooTPlayer request, the club/academy must be logged in on the YFTChain portal.

o Once club/academy are logged in they need to do click on “Manage FooTPlayer and on the new page click on “Add Player”.

o After that the club/academy will conduct search on the dialogue box/pop-up opened by providing details such as name, email ID and phone number of the player.

o YFTChain platform will search its database and find the closest player matching as per the details provided by the club/academy.

o If the detail of the player provided by club/academy matches with any player on the platform, it will show in the results with player name, position, category. If this is the player the club/academy wants to add, the club/academy will click on the "Add" button and an invitation will be sent to the player's account for acceptance and confirmation.

o If the details of the player provided in the search section by the club/academy does not match with any player on the YFTChain platform, the club/academy has the option to send a FooTPlayer invitation request.

o The FooTPlayer invitation request can simply be sent by clicking on the button “Send Invitation” provided in the search results dialogue box/popup.

o Now on the "Manage FooTPlayer" page, you can see the status of the invitation sent to each player.

o Once the player accepts the request, the player will be added to the club / academy and can be found on the "Club’s / Academy's FooTPlayer" tab and "Manage FooTPlayer" Tab
